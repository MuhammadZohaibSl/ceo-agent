# Market Analysis - B2B Analytics Industry

## Market Size
- Total Addressable Market (TAM): $85B globally
- Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): $12B (mid-market segment)
- Current Market Share: 0.23%

## Key Competitors
1. **Tableau (Salesforce)** - Market leader, enterprise focus, expensive
2. **Looker (Google)** - Strong cloud integration, complex setup
3. **Domo** - Direct competitor, similar pricing, less ML capabilities
4. **Sisense** - Growing fast, good embedded analytics

## Market Trends
- Shift to real-time analytics (30% CAGR)
- AI/ML integration becoming table stakes
- Demand for self-service analytics growing
- Data privacy regulations increasing complexity

## Geographic Opportunities
### Europe
- GDPR compliance is a differentiator
- UK market most mature, Germany fastest growing
- Entry cost: $2-3M for proper localization
- Timeline to profitability: 18-24 months

### Asia-Pacific
- Singapore as regional hub
- Japan requires local partnerships
- China complex due to regulations
- Entry cost: $3-5M
