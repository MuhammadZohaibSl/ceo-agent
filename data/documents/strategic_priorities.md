1. Achieve $50M ARR by end of 2026
2. Expand to European market (UK + Germany)

## Risk Appetite
- **Acceptable**: Moderate financial risk up to $5M per initiative
- **Unacceptable**: Regulatory violations, customer data breaches
- **Conservative on**: International expansion (max 2 new markets/year)
- **Aggressive on**: Product innovation and AI capabilities
